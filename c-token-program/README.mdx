1. c-Mints and c-Tokens are equivalents to SPL mints and tokens.
2. The key difference is c-Mint and c-Token accounts do not require rent-exemption upon creation.
3. c-Token accounts can hold token balances of c-Mints, SPL mints, or Token 2022 mints. SPL tokens can be converted to c-Tokens and back on demand.

|  Creation Cost         | SPL  | c-Token      |
|:---------------------|:------------------|:----------------------|
| **Mint Account**    | ~1,500,000 lamports        | **15,000** lamports     |
| **Token Account**   | 	  ~2,000,000 lamports         | ~**17,000** lamports     |
