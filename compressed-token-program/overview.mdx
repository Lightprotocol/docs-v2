1. c-Mints and c-Tokens are equivalents to SPL mints and tokens.
2. The key difference is c-Mints and c-Tokens do not require rent-exemption upon creation.

|  Creation Cost         | SPL  | c-Token      |
|:---------------------|:------------------|:----------------------|
| **Mint Account**    | 1,461,600 lamports        | 15,000 lamports     |
| **Token Account**   | 	2,039,280 lamports         | 22,288 lamports     |

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="" icon="coins">
  ## c-Mint Accounts
    * Uniquely represent a token and store its global metadata.
    * Mint address serves as the token's unique identifier.
    * c-Mints are compressed accounts and rent-free.
  </Card>
  <Card title="" icon="wallet">
  ## c-Token Accounts
    * Hold token balances of c-Mints, SPL mints, or Token 2022 mints.
    * SPL tokens can be converted to <br/>c-Tokens and back on demand.
    * Custom rent config reduces account creation cost.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
