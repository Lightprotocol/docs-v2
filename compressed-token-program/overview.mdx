## c-Mint Accounts

1. cMints uniquely represent a token on Solana and store its global metadata.
2. Tokens created from cMints are c-Tokens. The mint's address serves as the token's unique identifier.
3. cMints are compressed accounts and rent-free.

## c-Token Accounts

1. c-Token & associated c-Token accounts (c-ATAs) are **Solana accounts** that<br/> **hold balances** of **cMint, SPL mint, or Token 2022 mint** tokens.
2. Existing **SPL tokens can be converted** to c-Tokens and back on demand.
3. The compressible extension implements a **custom rent config**.
