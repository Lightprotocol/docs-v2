1. cToken & associated cToken accounts (cATAs) are Solana accounts that hold balances of tokens of cMints, SPL mints, or Token 2022 mints.
2. cToken accounts and cATAs are compressible with a default rent config that reduces creation cost.

|  | cToken account | SPL token account |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| creation cost | 22,288 lamports | 2,039,280 lamports |

## Account Struct

The layout of cToken accounts mirrors the SPL token account layout.
