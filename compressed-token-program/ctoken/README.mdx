1. cToken & associated cToken accounts (cATAs) are **Solana accounts** that<br/> **hold balances** of **cMint, SPL mint, or Token 2022 mint** tokens.
2. cToken accounts and cATAs are **compressible** that reduces account creation cost.

|  | cToken account | SPL token account |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| **Creation Cost** | 22,288 lamports | 2,039,280 lamports |
