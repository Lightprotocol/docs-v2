Define `CompressibleParamsInfos` with the standard rent config and accounts:
- Rent for 24 hours (~11,000 lamports)
- Top-up amount to 766 lamports (3h rent)

```rust
use light_compressed_token_sdk::ctoken::CompressibleParamsInfos;

let compressible_params = CompressibleParamsInfos::new(
    compressible_config.clone(),
    rent_sponsor.clone(),
    system_program.clone(),
);
```
