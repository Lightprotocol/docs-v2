1. Associated c-Token accounts (c-ATA) are Solana accounts that hold token balances of cMints, SPL mints, or Token 2022 mints.
2. The address for c-ATAs is deterministically derived with the owner's address, *c-Token program ID*, and mint address.
3. c-ATAs accounts are compressible with a default rent config.
