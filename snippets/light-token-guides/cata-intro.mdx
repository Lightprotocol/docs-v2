1. Associated light-token accounts (light-ATA) are Solana accounts that hold token balances of light-mints, SPL mints, or Token 2022 mints.
2. The address for light-ATAs is deterministically derived with the owner's address, *light-token program ID*, and mint address.
3. light-ATAs accounts are compressible with a default rent config.
