Solana has 3 main token programs: SPL-token, Token-2022, and c-token.

C-token is like SPL-token, but with 200x cheaper accounts.

## Performance Comparison

### Creating c-token accounts is 200x cheaper

| Operation | c-token | SPL |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| allocate | 0.000011 | 0.002 |
| keep hot for 24h | 0.000005 | - |

### c-token is 70%+ more compute efficient (CU) than SPL on hot paths

| Operation | c-token | SPL |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| transfer | 312 - 1,885 | ~4,000 |
| createAssociatedTokenAccount | ~4,300 | ~20,000 |
